## Who We Are

We are a GTM (go-to-market) agency specializing in developer tools and open source products.

Our focus: technical founders — solo developers and small teams (1-3 people) — who built a product but don't know how to bring it to market. They think like engineers. GTM is a blind spot for them. We speak their language and translate their product into market language.

We are NOT:
- A full-service marketing agency
- An SMM agency
- A "marketing for everyone" shop
- Ghostwriters who do the work instead of the founder

We ARE:
- A GTM diagnostics and strategy partner for early-stage tech products
- Async-first (Notion + Loom, no unnecessary calls)
- AI-leveraged (80%+ of analysis done with AI tools)

## Our First Product: GTM Strategy Audit

### What it is
A diagnostic of a startup's go-to-market strategy through our 5-phase GTM framework.

We take a startup that:
- Has a working product
- Has no clarity on how to go to market
- OR has tried things that didn't work

And answer: **why it's not working and exactly what to do.**

### What the client gets:
1. **GTM Diagnostic** — which of 5 phases they're stuck in and why
2. **ICP Analysis** — who the real buyer is (jobs-to-be-done, pain signals)
3. **Positioning Gap** — how they position now vs how they should
4. **Channel Recommendation** — top 2 channels for their stage and product type
5. **Messaging Framework** — how to talk about the product (landing page, cold outreach, social)
6. **30-Day Action Plan** — specific steps with success metrics
7. **Notion Report** — everything above in one shareable document
8. **Async Q&A** — 48 hours of follow-up questions after delivery

**Delivery time:** 1 week. Async. No long calls.

## Our GTM Framework (5 Phases)

We use this framework both for our clients and as a diagnostic tool:

- **Phase −1:** Diagnosis (ICP, jobs-to-be-done, positioning, go/no-go)
- **Phase 0:** Foundation (landing page, messaging toolkit, community setup)
- **Phase 1:** First Clients — manual mode (personal network, direct outreach, communities)
- **Phase 2:** Public Impulse (Show HN, Reddit, social media builder story)
- **Phase 3:** Content Series + Scalable Outreach
- **Phase 3.5:** Paid Amplification (optional, only after organic works)
- **Phase 4:** Product Hunt Launch
- **Phase 5:** Growth Loop (100+ clients, sustainable acquisition machine)

Every startup is stuck at one phase. Our audit identifies which one — and what exactly to do to move forward.
