## 1. Obsidian

## Cross-Competitor Insights for Brand Strategy

### Messaging territories that are OCCUPIED
| Phrase | Owner |
|---|---|
| "Sharpen your thinking" | Obsidian |
| "Think better" | Reflect |
| "Privacy-first knowledge base" | Logseq |
| "A studio for your mind" | Capacities |
| "It's not you. It's your tools." | Capacities |
| "Write, Draw, Plan" / "KnowledgeOS" | AFFiNE |
| "Remember everything" (voice) | Voicenotes |
| "Elevate your thinking" | Deta Surf |

### What NO ONE owns yet
- **Instant, zero-friction thought capture** (all competitors require opening an app or switching context)
- **"Your AI runs on YOUR machine"** — local LLM as default, not a setting or plugin
- **"No organization required"** — vector-based recall vs folder/tag/backlink systems
- **"Ask your memory"** — semantic recall framing (Voicenotes has "Ask AI" but cloud-based)
- **The combination:** fast capture + local AI + zero organization overhead

### What users keep complaining about across all tools
1. **Capture friction** — have to open the app, find the right note, decide where things go
2. **AI requires cloud** — every AI feature sends data somewhere (Reflect → OpenAI, Voicenotes → cloud, Capacities → cloud AI)
